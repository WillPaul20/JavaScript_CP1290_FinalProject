Helpful Material:

The object of the game is to clear the board without selecting any mines. There will be anywhere from 5, 10 (Default), or 20 mines based on the difficulty button selected.

Left Click to select a grid square.

Right Click to flag a grid square.

Right Click flagged grid square to unflag.
